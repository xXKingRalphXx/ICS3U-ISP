THE RULES
--------------------------------------------------

Player 1's Pieces
    'r1' represents player one's regular pieces
Player 2's Pieces
    'r2' represents player two's regular pieces
    'k2' represents player two's king pieces

Both regular and king pieces can move only one space at a time
Both regular and king pieces can move two spaces if executing a capture move
    That is, 'checking' another piece

Regulars CANNOT MOVE BACKWARDS â€” that ability is reserved for kings ALONE
